January

1814. Jan. 1. Sa. Write to Miller A S, & Day. Call on Panton, Lett & Hamond: seek Rayre. Hamond calls.


[fol. 11v]

Jan. 2. Su.Expect Gell: call on Place: Lambs sup. Raised from great depression, by F Pe.

3. M.Write to Patrickson. Call on Price, Macmillan, Hume & Knowles: seek Rayre: Gell calls n.

4. Tu.GWrite to Day, & Miller, Perth: Drayton, Mortimer, st. 35. Gell, & Jones, Size Lane, call: call on Taylor (Gray’s Inn), Ellis (Museum) & Place.

5. W. Write to Greenhill. Barons’ Wars, C. I. Call on Rayre, Mills, Jeffery, Thane, Bentham, Sutherland, Haughton & Knowles: meet capt. Curran: M dines: Hogan & R Morrel at tea. Seek Spackmn & Segur.

6. Th.Write to Rayre. Call on Place & Hume: Perry engr calls: Hogan at tea.

7. F. Barons’ Wars, 1/2 C. II: Mornay, çala. Call on Egerton, Price & Perry engr: Hamond dines; adv. Perry: H Robinson calls.

8. Sa. Barons’ War, C. II. Call on Cherry: Booth dines: John Evans (Bristol) au soir.


[fol. 12r]

Jan. 9. M.eBarons’ Wars, 1/2 C. III: Mornay, p. 22. Booth dines; adv. Hamond, Waller, & Evans (Bristol): call on Place.

10. Tu. eBaron’s Wars, C. III. Call on Howard (w. Hayward), Hume, Knowles, Place twice, & Cobbet: Gell & Booth calln: Hamond at tea: Sutton calls. Write to Miller A S.

11. W .eBarons’ Wars, 1/2 C. IV: Mornay, p. 43. Call on Davison: Faulder atty & Hogan call: Booth dines.

12. Th.eMornay, c. 7: Astrophel, çala. Call on Gell, Price & Davison: Booth, W Curran, Lambert, Gell & Hamond call.

13. F .eBarons’ Wars, C. IV, V: 1/3 Edward II. Call on Price: seek King: Hamond calls: Booth dines.

14. F. Barons’ Wars, 1/2 C. VI. Faithful & Faulder atty call: call on King, Price & Haywardn: Booth at tea.

15. Sa.Barons’ Wars, fin. Call on Place: Booth calls.


[fol. 12v]

Jan. 16. Su. Drayton, Ep. 1, 2: Mornay, c. 11, 12. Call on Perry: Booth dines.

17. M. Drayton, Ep. 3. Call on King, Sutherland, Curtis & Hume: Booth, L Kenney & Hogan at tea.

18. Tu.Drayton, Ep. 4, 5: Mornay, c. 13. Call on Davison, Price, Taylor (Gray’s Inn), Hume, Perry & Place.

19. W.Drayton, Ep. 6. Call on Jas White: seek Ballachey: Hamond calls: Booth dines: Rylance calls.

20. Th.Write to C C, Harwich. Drayton, Ep. 7. Meet Nicholson: call on Place: M dines.

21. F. Write to Shelley, & C C, Landguard Fort. Drayton, Ep. 8. Call on Ballachey: seek Buxton: Booth &Hamond call. News of S. leaving Londone.

22. Sa.Write to Shelley. Call on Curtis, Davison, Hazard, White & R Taylor: seek Price: Gill & R Taylor call: theatre, w. M J & W, Coriolanuse: C C at home; adv. Booth. adv. W Curran & Steele.


[fol. 13r]

Jan. 23. Su.Shelley expected: Booth dines; adv. Lambs.

24. M. Call on Price & Ballachey: Hogan, Faulder atty, Ridgway jr, M & L K call, & Jo G: Kenney dines: call on Place: meet Newton & Shelley.

25. Tu.Drayton, Ep. 13, 14. Letter to Taylor, Norwich. M & Perry artist call: call on King (Adephi) & Hume.

26. W. Call on Davison twice, Critchit, Theobald, Ward & Middleton, & bill-brokers: Booth dines: Gill calls.

27. Th.Write to Taylor (Norwich), Shelley & Day. Call on Davison, & Sutherland, w. M J & Perry: Hamond & Hogan call: call on Dufour, w. Gill, M Lamb (adv. M Burney) & Emlyn.

28. F. Patronage, p. 210. Call on Rickm[ansn?] & Knaptonbbs,R Sharp, & Crosbyn: Perry artist & R Crosby call.

29. Sa.Patronage, p. 418. Call on Price, Ballachey & Sweetman.


[fol. 13v]

Jan. 30. Su.Patronage, Vol. II, p. 76. Call on Plce, w. C C: S E dines; invités Kennies, Currans & Hamond.

31. M.Patronage, p. 144. Pay Mackintosh. Call on Middleton & Ballachey: M calls: Hogan at tea; adv. H Robinson.
