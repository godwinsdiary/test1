February

Feb. 1. M.Malthus, p. 34. Ariosto, C. III, st. 10. Hone, Longdil & T T call: call on Hayward.

2. Tu.Ariosto, st. 25. Rodd’s. Write to Longdil. Trial for Damagesee.Frost & snow. Giddinesse.

3. W. Malthus, çala. House of Esté, p. 88. Hazlit sups.

4. Th.Malthus, p. 36/2. Call on J Taylor: Rosser calls, ppc. Call on Lambn.

5. F. Malthus, p. 38. Ogilvie calls: call on Hayward: Booth dines.

6. Sa. Malthus, p. 40/2. Write to Fairley. Oldcastle, acts 1, 2, 3. Rodd’s.


[fol. 33v]

Feb. 7. Su. Malthus, p. 42. Ariosto, st. 40.

8. M. Malthus, p. 44, & 1 page. N G calls: 4 Baxters dine; adv. T T & T Ht.

9. Tu. Malthus, çala. York, act 1. Baron sups.

10. W. Malthus, 2 pp. Theatre, Evadnee.

11. Th. Malthus, p. 47. Write to Livorno. Ogilvie & Hayward call.

12. F.Malthus, p. 50. Write to Livorno. Irving, Ogilvie, Hazlit, Hume & Booth dine; invités T T, Mulready & Baron.

13. Sa. Malthus, p. 53/2. Recollections of Curran, çala. Sotheby’s; adv. Heber & White: Rodd’s.


[fol. 34r]

Feb. 14. Su.Malthus, p. 55/2. Ariosto, st. 60. Hayward & T T call: call on Perry; adv. Hill.

15. M. Malthus, p. 58. Call on J P Collier n & M Lamb: theatre, Switzerlande. Hobhouse’s Poll begins, 15 dayse.

16. Tu. Jo G breakfasts: call on Booth: N G calls: Rodd’s.

17. W. Smith, çala. Ogilvie calls: meet Hayward.

18. Th.Smith, çala. Write to Constable. Booth calls: dine at Snow’s, w. mrs Austin, 2 Beresfords, M J & S P.

19. F.Smith, çala. Ariosto, st. 77; C. IV, st. 13. Hayward, Otis &Blatterman call: meet H Robinson.

20. Sa.Malthus, p. 60. Ariosto, st. 26. Fearon calls.


[fol. 34v]

Feb. 21. Su.Malthus, p. 62. Ariosto, st. 40. Hayward & T Rodd call: call on John Lamb, w. M J & S P.

22. M. Malthus, p. 65/2. W Curran calls n: call on H Robinsonn, Wallace & Ogilvie: Booth at tea: theatre, Heroine; adv. Talfourde.

23. Tu.Malthus, p. 66/2. Ariosto, st. 72. Hayward, Ogilvien; Hume & Botwright call: Rodd’s. T Ht sups.

24. W. Smith, çala. Ariosto, C. V, st. 13. Beresfords call: dine at Baxter’s, w. Booths, Sharp, M J, S P & W. Snow.

25. Th.Malthus, 68/2. Nash, &c. Call on Northcote; adv. mrs Kean, mrs Cox: T T at tea.

26. T. Malthus, p. 68. Ariosto, st. 28.

27. Sa.Ariosto, st. 38. Nash, Apologie, çala. Hayward, Tate & Botwright call: H Robinson sups.


[fol. 35r]

Feb. 28. Su. Ariosto, st. 53. Nash, &c. W Curran dines; adv. H Robinson.
